Who

Background

The Middle Brain offers digital user-centric experiences on the web, on the go, around communities, and around brands. From Concept ideation and branding to development of design, and programming. From web based platforms and mobile, to Digital Marketing, UX, and Social Media.

The Middle Brain serves a wide range of industries such as e-commerce, health, wellness and treatment, finance, education and fashion.

The Middle Brain is used to work globally with different time zones, cultures and languages, while passionate about creativity, communication, collaboration and diversity of ideas.

Clients include: Harvard University, Sears, The NYC Charter Schools Center, Snapple, The New-York University, Tupperware and Rosenthal, Germany.

Lia Aran

Creative Director, Owner

Lia Aran had provided creative leadership, and user centric solutions for some of the major brands such as Ford, Comcast, Bed Bath & Beyond, GM, Athena Health, Sharp and Texaco.

With over fifteen years of experience in Digital media, Lia had the opportunity to work at some of the leading agencies and brands in New-York such as Razorfish, Agency.com, the Knot, and lectured on Brand identity at The New York University for over two years.

Lia established a successful career in her native country Israel leading creative for major brands such as MasterCard, and the Center of Educational Technology, and creating digital games and marketing experiences for the European market (Everyman’s Library, London and Swatch).

Lia graduated with a BFA from Bezalel Academy of Art and Design, Jerusalem, Israel, in Visual Communication.
